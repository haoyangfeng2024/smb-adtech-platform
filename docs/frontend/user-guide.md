# SMB Platform User Guide

Welcome to the SMB AdTech Platform! This guide will help you navigate the interface and launch your first AI-optimized advertising campaign.

## 1. Understanding Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is your command center for monitoring advertising performance across the open web.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Impressions**: The total number of times your ad was displayed to a potential customer.
- **Clicks**: The number of times users interacted with your ad.
- **Total Spend**: The actual budget consumed by your campaigns in real-time.
- **ROI (Return on Investment)**: The estimated value generated compared to your ad spend. Our **Privacy Measurement Engine** calculates this using probabilistic attribution to protect user privacy.

### Performance Charts
- **Trend Analysis**: View how your clicks and conversions fluctuate over time.
- **ML Insights**: Brief summaries generated by our AI Assistant explaining *why* certain trends are occurring (e.g., "Increased mobile traffic in Texas led to a 15% ROI boost").

## 2. Launching a Campaign

Creating a campaign is a streamlined 3-step process designed for busy business owners.

### Step 1: Basic Information
- **Campaign Name**: Give your campaign a descriptive name (e.g., "Spring Coffee Promotion").
- **Business Description**: Describe what you are selling. This helps our **AI Assistant** generate relevant ad copy for you.

### Step 2: Budget & Bidding
- **Daily Budget**: The maximum amount you want to spend each day.
- **Total Budget**: The cap for the entire campaign duration.
- **AI Bidding Strategy**: By default, our **PPO RL Agent** will manage your bids to maximize ROI. You can choose to be "Aggressive" for reach or "Conservative" for efficiency.

### Step 3: Audience & Targeting
- **Target Audience**: Describe your ideal customer. Our **GNN Ad Graph** will translate this into anonymized audience segments.
- **Geographic Targeting**: Select specific U.S. states or regions to focus your spend.

## 3. Privacy & Compliance

Your data and your customers' privacy are our top priority.
- **Anonymized Processing**: No personal data (PII) is ever stored on our servers. All targeting is based on encrypted, aggregated signals.
- **Compliance Shield**: The platform is fully compliant with CCPA, GDPR, and the latest U.S. privacy standards.
